About US

Stouhi Steel is a Lebanese-based company located in Beirut and Jamous, which specializes in supplying a variety of glass, aluminum, and metal works. Some of these works include steel CNC machining, casting, forging, and hardware and material supplying. Along with the lengthy experience in the Middle East, Stouhi Steel is an exclusive agent for several international companies that specialize in manufacturing door/gate motors and locks, amongst other fields of works
